Frequently asked questions

Who are these certifications for?

Anyone building AI-ready skills — from non-technical professionals starting with Fundamentals programs to engineers and leaders pursuing Practitioner, Expert and Strategist tiers.

What's the difference between the tiers?

Fundamentals build awareness, Practitioner programs build applied job skills, Specialist programs cover niche roles, and Expert/Strategist tiers are advanced and leadership-focused.

How are exams conducted?

Exams are online and AI-proctored, combining multiple-choice questions, scenarios and practical items. You'll know your pass threshold before you begin.

Are the credentials verifiable?

Yes. Every credential has a unique ID that anyone can confirm through the ITHR verification portal.

Do I need a technical background?

Not for Fundamentals programs — they're written in plain language for everyone. Higher tiers assume progressively more hands-on experience.
